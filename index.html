These experiences fostered a deeper understanding of international cooperation, cultural exchange, and the crucial role of diplomacy in shaping a more peaceful and interconnected world.

Favorites

Foods

My favorite food is Humba. Humba holds a special place among my favorite Filipino dishes. This savory braised pork dish, with its rich flavors of soy sauce, vinegar, and sweet notes, is simply irresistible. The tender meat, infused with the aroma of garlic and bay leaves, melts in your mouth. Humba is a true comfort food, evoking warm memories of family gatherings and festive occasions. Whether served with steaming white rice or enjoyed with a side of vegetables, Humba never fails to satisfy my cravings.

Movie

My favorite movie is "Wicked." The "Wicked" movie, starring the phenomenal duo of Cynthia Erivo and Ariana Grande, was a visual and auditory feast. Erivo's powerful vocals and nuanced portrayal of Elphaba, the misunderstood "Wicked Witch of the West," were simply breathtaking. Grande, as Glinda, brought a captivating blend of sweetness and strength to the role. The film's stunning visuals, extravagant costumes, and breathtaking musical numbers transported me to the magical world of Oz. "Wicked" is a cinematic triumph that masterfully reimagines this beloved story, leaving a lasting impression with its poignant themes of friendship, prejudice, and the complexities of good and evil.

Manhua/Manhwa/Manga

My favorite manhua is "Solo Leveling" by Chugong (추공), who wrote the original web novel, while the primary illustrator for the webtoon adaptation was Jang Sung-Rak (장성락), also known as "DUBU".. Solo Leveling is my absolute favorite manhua. The story follows Sung Jin-woo, an ordinary man who inexplicably gains the ability to level up at an unprecedented rate after a near-death experience. Witnessing his journey from a weak hunter to an unstoppable force is incredibly exhilarating. The action sequences are breathtakingly drawn, and the power system is both unique and captivating. Solo Leveling masterfully blends elements of action, fantasy, and even a touch of dark fantasy, creating an immersive and unforgettable reading experience.
